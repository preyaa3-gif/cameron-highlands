History & Overview

Cameron Highlands is a popular hill station in Malaysia, famous for its tea plantations, cool weather, and lush greenery. It was discovered by William Cameron in 1885 and has since become a premier retreat.

Why Visit Cameron Highlands?

Feature Description
Cool Climate Escape the tropical heat with refreshing mountain air.
Scenic Tea Plantations Enjoy breathtaking views, and learn about tea production.
Strawberry Picking Experience handpicking fresh strawberries at local farms.
Mystical Forests Explore the Mossy Forest, rich in biodiversity and misty landscapes.
Colorful Gardens Visit the Lavender Garden, Rose Valley, and Butterfly Garden.
Local Delicacies Savor fresh produce, tea, and homemade strawberry desserts.

Top Attractions

  • Boh Tea Plantation: Enjoy panoramic views and tea processing.
  • Mossy Forest: A mystical forest with unique flora and cool misty ambiance.
  • Strawberry Farms: Pick fresh strawberries and taste delicious strawberry-based products.
  • Lavender Garden: A beautiful garden filled with vibrant flowers and photo spots.

Climate & Weather

The highlands enjoy a cool climate with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 25°C, making it a refreshing escape from Malaysia’s tropical heat.
