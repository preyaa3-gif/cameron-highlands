An Ancient Cloud Forest

The Mossy Forest is a unique "Cloud Forest" ecosystem estimated to be over 200 million years old.

Ecological Facts:

  • Vegetation: Trees are covered in thick layers of moss, lichen, and rare orchids.
  • Wildlife: It is a habitat for carnivorous Pitcher Plants (Nepenthes).
  • Conservation: Visitors must stay on the boardwalk to protect sensitive root systems.

Known as one of Malaysia’s oldest ecosystems, the Mossy Forest is a prehistoric "Cloud Forest" where misty boardwalks lead you past 200-million-year-old trees blanketed in thick moss and rare carnivorous pitcher plants.
