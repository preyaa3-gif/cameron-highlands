A Sea of Purple

The Cameron Lavender Garden is a beautifully landscaped floral attraction that features various species of lavender and other colorful flowers.

Essential Facts:

  • Variety: Although famous for purple lavender, the garden also features massive displays of Gerbera Daisies and Camellias.
  • Unique Treats: The garden is the only place where you can try the famous Lavender-flavored soft serve ice cream.
  • Photography: The site is designed with tiered walkways specifically to offer the best angles for photography.

Walking through the garden, you will notice that it is much more than just flowers. It is a full sensory experience of scents and colors.
