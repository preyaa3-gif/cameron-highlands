Sweet Harvest Experience

One of the most popular activities in Cameron Highlands is visiting the local strawberry farms, where the cool climate allows these fruits to thrive year-round.

Essential Facts:

  • Self-Picking: Many farms offer a "pick-your-own" experience where you can harvest fresh berries directly from the vine.
  • Hydroponics: Most farms use vertical hydroponic systems to maximize space and keep the fruit clean.
  • Variety: Aside from fresh berries, these farms are famous for strawberry jam, ice cream, and juice.

The best time to visit is during the peak season from May to August, though you can find fresh produce at any time of the year.
